COLORZILLA

What is ColorZilla?

It is an extension for Google Chrome or Firefox that assists the web developers to pick up a color, so when you are browsing and your eys fall on an interesting color now you can just with a click pick it.

How it works?

at first you should install it depending on which browser you use than from Google Chrome extensions you can add it as extension,after all just click on it and choose 'Page Color Picker Active' then you can move your mouse where the color that you want is and then you will get above the color value in Hex and in rgb.

first of all you should install it and add it to the extensions of your browser than you can freely use it, when you click on it from your extension it will take a screeshoot from the page that you choosed and then just by draging the mouse on the box for example that you want to know its heigh and width and you will get above the values and for the color picker you can find it in the tool bar on the left side by clicking on it just by moving your mouse on the color that you want to pick it up and you will get above the value easily.
