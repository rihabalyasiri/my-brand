COLORZILLA

What is ColorZilla?

It is an extension for Google Chrome or Firefox that assists the web developers to pick up a color, so when you are browsing and your eys fall on an interesting color now you can just with a click pick it.

How it works?

Frst, you should install it in your browser of choice through its extension manager. Then simply choose 'Page Color Picker Active' you can hover your mouse over your chosen color and you will see a color value in Hex and RGB.

PIXEL ZOOMER
p>What is Pixelzoomer?

It is an web browser extension which can get distance measurements of the boxes, pictures or whatever you are working on. It also has the ability to pick up color values.

How it works?

Add it to the extensions of your browser. This extension will take a screenshot of the current website. Then for example, just by draging the mouse on the box you want to know the height and width of, the values will appear just above.

The Color Picker can be found on the left side of the toolbar. To select a color, click on the Color Picker and then move your mouse cursor onto the color you want and the value will appear.
